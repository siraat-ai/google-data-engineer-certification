# Professional Data Engineer Exam & Study Resources — Simple Notes

## Introduction
**Mr. X:** I’m taking this course about the Professional Data Engineer role at Cymbal Retail. What is the main purpose of this course?  
**Mr. Artificial King:** Great question! This course helps you understand the **Professional Data Engineer job role** and prepares you for the **certification exam** by showing you what you already know and what you still need to study.

## Understanding the Exam Guide
**Mr. X:** What is this “exam guide” everyone talks about?  
**Mr. Artificial King:** The exam guide is the official document that explains **what topics are covered in the certification exam**.  
- It is divided into **five sections**  
- Each section has **one or more objectives**  
- These objectives define the skills you’re expected to know  

You can always find the **latest version** of the exam guide on the certification page.

## Course vs Exam – Important Clarification
**Mr. X:** Will the course tell me the exact exam questions?  
**Mr. Artificial King:** No, and that’s very important to understand.  
- The **exam questions** are created by a different team  
- The **course creators** do not know the real exam questions  
- The course’s job is to **help you assess your knowledge**, not predict questions  

The goal is to help you **build a study plan** and get ready for both the **job role** and the **exam**.

## Diagnostic Questions & Study Plan
**Mr. X:** How do I know what I should study more?  
**Mr. Artificial King:** Throughout the course, you’ll answer **diagnostic questions**.  
These questions:
- Show what you already understand  
- Highlight **knowledge gaps**  
- Help you decide **what to study next**

Based on your answers, the course points you to helpful **resources and documentation**.
