# Knowledge Check: Preparing and Using Data for Analysis

## Securely Sharing Data with External Partners
**Mr. X:** We need to share inventory data from Cymbal Retail with a partner company. They also use BigQuery. How can we share the data securely and efficiently?  
**Mr. Artificial King:** When both organizations use BigQuery, the best solution is **Analytics Hub**. It allows you to share datasets directly without copying data, while maintaining strong access control and governance.

**✔ Correct Answer:** Analytics Hub

## Supporting the Machine Learning Team
**Mr. X:** Cymbal Retail has ML engineers who build and maintain models. As a Professional Data Engineer, how do I support them?  
**Mr. Artificial King:** Your primary responsibility is around **data**, not model tuning. You support ML engineers by:
- Processing raw data  
- Cleaning and transforming it  
- Preparing high-quality datasets for **feature engineering**

Good data is the foundation of good machine learning.

**✔ Correct Answer:** Process and prepare existing data to enable feature engineering

## Key Takeaways
**Mr. Artificial King:** Remember:
- Use **Analytics Hub** to share BigQuery data securely with partners  
- Data Engineers enable ML success by delivering **clean, well-prepared data**  

When data is ready, ML teams can focus on building better models—and the business benefits faster.
